HOW WILL TOMORROW LOOK?

What are the implications of all of us living to age 120 and beyond?

1
Equality
Will we allow an increased lifespan to influence financial equality and democracy in the world??
2
Environment
Will we finally start caring properly about the planet now that we understand we’ll be here for more than 120 years?
3
Personal
Will we change our mindset about career, relationships and a free will to die with our extended, healthy lifespan?
What can we do right now?
We do not have the answers yet to the moral issues related to extending human lifespan, but there are some things we can start doing right away
1
Change your mindset
Start thinking about what you would do if you’d live to a 100, 120, or even 150 years old. What would you do differently for yourself? For others? For the planet?
Start today
If we’ll all be on this planet for 150 years, we better make it a great place. If it’s planting a tree or stopping using plastic, it doesn’t matter what you do, just start today, not tomorrow.
